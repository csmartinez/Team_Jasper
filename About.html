About the Project


Team Jasper is a capstone team at Northern Arizona University that aims to develop the JASS virtual training space that will allow education faculty to create and deploy individually-customized classroom simulations, which are then used as the basis for a series of training exercises that allow education majors to gain hands-on experience with diversity issues they will typically face within the a diverse classroom.
